TABLE: labels
├── id (PK, Integer)
├── product_name (String, unique)
├── form (FK to forms, String) 
├── price - general price
├── unit_price (Float) - price per unit
├── created_at (DateTime)
└── mark_to_print (Boolean) 

TABLE: forms
├── name(PK, String) - e.g. - "capsule, tablet, solution, pulveres,..."
├── short_name (String) e.g. - "plv","ung","cps",..
└── unit (String) - e.g. - "g","ml","pcs",..
